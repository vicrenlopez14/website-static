About us ProFInd

The temporary hiring of professionals and services, safely

ProFind

Can be described as a software that is aimed at making the temporary hiring of professionals by individuals more efficient and comfortable, making the monitoring by the client of the situation that their processes have with the hired professionals easier and faster, this is made up of a web application and a desktop application.

The desktop application

Will have three different views, both for the client, the administrator and the professional. The client's view would open up the project itself, this would include the process of agreeing terms, contract, professional remuneration, as well as the monitoring of processes that would be carried out in the project agreed by the client.

Frequent questions

ProFind is a company whose mission is to contract temporary services and to ensure this service from start to finish.

It will be possible to obtain the services with a desktop application that will have many tools while shielding better options for the client.

The application will be for the contracting of the services that you want, you can also be notified of your service as it goes and you can make your payments from the application.

A much faster method of contracting service, having real-time information on how your service is going.

It can only be downloaded from the Microsoft store.
