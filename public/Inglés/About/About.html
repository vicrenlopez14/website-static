Introduction

ProFind proposes a system for hiring professionals, in its minimum version should have the ability to hire, manage work processes and interact with them in the following areas:

1. Law firm.
2. Automotive services.
3. General medicine.

The solution seeks to provide alternatives to small individuals when they need services that, in the case of a small company, its staff does not cover; or, if it is a private individual, it requires it temporarily.

Objetive

The objective of our company is to have an easier contracting for our clients and to be able to ensure 100% security of the contract from start to finish and to be able to count on the best professionals you will find in the country.

Mission and Vision of the company

Vision

To become the best and most recognized option for finding professionals to be hired on a temporary basis, boosting their reputation, as well as ensuring the success of the projects entrusted to them.


Mission

Our mission is to connect professionals with potential clients, facilitating the time needed to get projects off the ground. In turn, facilitating interfaces for planning, verification and remuneration.
